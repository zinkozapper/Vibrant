A Comparison of Joan of Arc & The Book of Mormon

The Maid of Orléans

Joan of Arc (c. 1412–1431) was less than 20 years old when she became commander of the French military. A simple peasant girl, she was guided by celestial voices leading to miraculous victories for the French during the Hundred Years' War. Without her, France today and potentially America would not exist.

There are many parallels between her life and those found within the Book of Mormon. She exemplfies following the influence of the Spirit and following correct principles.
